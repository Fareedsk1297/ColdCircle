About-Us

Our Founder, Paul N. Nasrani found his passion for ice cream on a small Pennsylvania farm during summers of his youth. At the end of a long day, he could always find the energy to help churn a batch of fresh homemade ice cream. The flavors, taste, and quality were unrivalled and Paul’s dream of creating a company to share this wonderful ice cream with the world was hatched. Paul’s professional career began in accounting and finance in New York City and he eventually became CFO of a mid-size corporation. However, the ice cream dream never left him and in 2002 he began producing homemade premium ice creams in a tiny Manhattan studio apartment. He produced far more than he could eat himself and began to share it with co-workers and friends. The response was tremendous and soon a much larger ice cream freezer was necessary to meet demand. To learn more about ice cream production, Paul spent his vacations visiting every ice cream shop he could find to speak with the proprietor and attending dairy programs at career to make ice cream in the North Country and pursue his dream of sharing his ice cream with the world.

So Delicious has been bringing you dairy-free deliciousness—decadent frozen desserts, melty cheese alternatives, creamy yogurt alternatives, smooth plant-based beverages and flavorful coffee creamers—for over 30 years. But being dairy-free goes beyond deliciousness. We believe people have the power to eat good, feel good, and do good, which is why we’re committed to transforming mindful ingredients into mind-blowing delicious products that do right by people and the planet. It’s about thoughtful choices around everything we do; from sourcing ingredients and packaging to equality in company policies. We call it #MoreThanTheMinimum. #MoreThanTheMinimum is why we use organic coconuts. It’s why all of our products are certified vegan and Non-GMO Project Verified. It’s why we pay more than the minimum wage to all our employees. And it’s why we’re proud to be part of the B Corp™ movement.
